About Our Institute

Covenant Bible Institute of theology is affiliated to Presbyterian Church in Uganda within the Easter Presbytery and it offers certificate and Diploma programs in theology and other disciplines. It is now the centre of theological education for the One Hundred Ten churches in the Eastern Presbytery which extends to the five EAST AFRICAN countries. CBIT is open to other denomination for admission of student for training.

Covenant Bible Institute of Theology was established by Pr. Maurice Ogenga in 1999 for the public benefit and it is recognized globally. Throughout our great history, CBIT has offered access to a wide range of academic opportunities. As one of the leading Institutes in higher education of theology, the Institution has pioneered change in the sector.
